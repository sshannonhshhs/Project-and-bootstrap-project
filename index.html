About Chicken Nation

Our Story, Our Passion

From Humble Beginnings

What began as a small family-run kitchen with one fryer and a dream, is today one of the most loved fried chicken destinations in the country. At Chicken Nation, we remain committed to the same homemade flavors that got us started, blending tradition with culinary creativity.

Milestones

  • 2015: First pop-up launched at a weekend fair in Chennai.
  • 2017: Opened our flagship outlet with 3 signature items.
  • 2020: Crossed 1 million orders and introduced air-fried chicken range.
  • 2023: Voted Best Local Fried Chicken Brand by Foodies India.
